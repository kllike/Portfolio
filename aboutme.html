About Me

My journey into the world of programming began in elementary school when I participated in a small workshop where we had the opportunity to write some code. That experience ignited my interest in software development, and ever since then, I have been eager to explore and expand my skills in this field.

Beyond my studies, I have a deep passion for creativity. My hobbies include drawing, primarily graphite and charcoal pencil portraits, photography, gaming, and baking. These activities allow me to express myself artistically and balance my technical interests with creativity.

Throughout my educational journey, I have actively pursued opportunities to improve my technical skills and participate in competitions (Hackathon).
