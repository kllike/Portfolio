My Drwaings

My drawing journey started before I was even in kindergarten. Up until 5th grade, I did more doodling than actual drawing. Then I decided to try improving my drawing skills by watching videos and tutorials on how to improve my work. I started with simple anime/cartoon-style drawings using different character references. Then I focused on improving my shading technique. About five years ago, I began trying to draw realistic portraits and objects. My next goal is to try drawing realistic animals and eventually move to colored drawings.

Photography/Editing

Photography and photo editing have always been very interesting to me. As with drawing, I started taking pictures at a young age. I began by photographing nature, animals, and people with my phone. Only a few years ago, my aunt gave me her old camera to start practicing with a real camera. Currently, I am focusing on researching and learning the right settings, lighting, and shooting techniques. Then, I plan to practice what I have learned.
